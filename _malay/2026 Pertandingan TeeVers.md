<p><strong>Our Rakan Bahasa students also participated enthusiastically in Pertandingan TeeVer’s, a podcast competition organised by the Malay Language Council, Singapore. Through this exciting experience, students explored the importance of Adab Sapaan dan Panggilan within the Malay community in a fun and creative way. They thoroughly enjoyed bringing the script to life as they voiced characters in a story about a ghost who tried to teach two siblings the importance of using respectful terms of address and greetings. The competition gave students the opportunity to strengthen their speaking and expressive reading skills while deepening their appreciation for Malay culture and values.</strong>
