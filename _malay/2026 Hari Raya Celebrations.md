<p>The Malay Department organized a Hari Raya Concert on 31st March 2026
to celebrate the month of Syawal. The event was lively and enjoyable, featuring
enthusiastic student participation in the Dikir Barat performance, parent
volunteers joining in the sing-along, and captivating performances by the
Malay dancers.</p>
