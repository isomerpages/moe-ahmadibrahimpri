<p>On 6 April, our Rakan Bahasa students proudly attended the Rakan Bahasa
Appointment Ceremony, where they took their oath together with fellow Rakan
Bahasa ambassadors from primary schools, secondary schools, and junior
colleges across Singapore. The ceremony was a meaningful occasion that
celebrated their commitment to promoting and appreciating the Malay language.</p>

<p>Students also enjoyed an engaging and interactive segment conducted by
the bangsawan troupe, Opera Academy, where they learnt valuable tips on
expressive and effective reading. One of our students, brave Zayyan, confidently
volunteered to read on stage, making the experience even more memorable
for our 2 Rakan Bahasa.</p>
