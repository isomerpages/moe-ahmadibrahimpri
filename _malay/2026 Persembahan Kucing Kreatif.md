<p>On 15th May, our Primary 3 students were treated to an engaging Drama
Showcase featuring actors and actresses from Adi Concepts, who brought
the story Kucing Kreatif to life through an entertaining and interactive
performance. Through the showcase, students learnt valuable lessons about
resilience, creativity, and the importance of thinking wisely when facing
challenges. The story also cleverly infused elements from the well-known
legend of Singapura Dilanggar Todak, which students had previously explored
in the Primary 3 Cita syllabus. The meaningful performance helped students
deepen their understanding of the story while making learning more lively,
memorable, and enjoyable.</p>
