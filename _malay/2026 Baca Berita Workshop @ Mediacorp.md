<p>Our Rakan Bahasa students also had the opportunity to participate in Baca
Berita, a Broadcast Journalism workshop held at Mediacorp Studios. It was
an enriching and memorable experience as students gained valuable insights
into the world of news reporting and presentation. They were especially
excited to meet and learn from Suria news presenters, Nity Farhanah and
Izwan Othman, who shared useful tips on confident speech, pronunciation,
and effective news delivery. Through this engaging workshop, students developed
a deeper appreciation for broadcast journalism and the importance of clear
and confident communication.</p>
