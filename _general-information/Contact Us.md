<p><strong>Communication Hours</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Parents are advised to contact our staff during school hours (7:30 am
– 5:00 pm), Monday to Friday during term time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teachers are not expected to respond to queries outside these hours, on
weekends and during school holidays.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teachers are not required to share their personal mobile numbers or communicate
via social media platforms.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For urgent messages during school hours, please contact the General Office
at 6759 2906, as teachers may be in class or unavailable.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Our Commitment:</strong>
</p>
<p>Your query is important to us, and you can expect responses within:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>3 working days</strong> for
simple queries</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>7 working days </strong>for
queries requiring some fact-finding</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>21 working days </strong>for
complex queries involving detailed investigations, multiple parties, or
external agencies</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Respectful Communication</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>We encourage open and respectful dialogue to support students’ well-being
and learning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>By showing mutual courtesy and respect, we set a positive example for
our children.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The school will disengage from any communication that is rude, disrespectful,
or abusive towards staff.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>MOE values our staff&nbsp;and will take firm action to protect them against
unreasonable behavior, harassment, or abuse.</p>
</li>
</ul>
