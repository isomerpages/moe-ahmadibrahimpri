<h4>A Heartfelt Tribute to Our AIPS Teachers by PSG on Teachers' Day</h4>
<p>This year's Teachers' Day celebration at AIPS was extra special, thanks
to the Parents Support Group. In a wonderful display of appreciation, our
dedicated PSG members teamed up with their children to present a lively
and heartfelt dance performance. The joy and energy shared on stage was
infectious, creating a memorable moment for everyone present. Our teachers
were all smiles, clearly touched by the thoughtful gesture.</p>
<p>A big thank you to our PSG for putting in the time and effort to make
this celebration so meaningful. Your performance was truly a highlight
of the day!</p>
