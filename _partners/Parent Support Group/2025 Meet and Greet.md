<p>On 13 February 2025, we warmly welcomed new parents to join the AIPS Parent
Support Group. Our Chairman delivered a welcome speech and shared important
information about the school's safety measures. The Exco team took turns
introducing the roles and responsibilities involved in various duties.
It was a wonderful opportunity for our new parents to mingle and connect
with our senior PSG members.</p>
