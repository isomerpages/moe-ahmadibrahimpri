<h4>Parent-Child Bonding Event for Primary 1: </h4>
<p>A Wonderful Collaboration with PSG</p>
<p>This year, we were delighted to collaborate with our dedicated Parents
Support Group to host a special Parent-Child Bonding event for our Primary
1 students. This memorable event was organised to celebrate our Primary
1 students' successful completion of their first milestone in their educational
journey.</p>
<p>The day kicked off with an exciting Nerf gun game, where parents teamed
up with their children to complete various missions set by the host master.
It was heartwarming to see the strong teamwork and the playful rivalry
between parents and their little ones. The excitement and laughter were
infectious, creating unforgettable memories for everyone involved.</p>
<p>Following the action-packed game, parents and children participated in
a creative craft workshop, where they learned to sew a handmade Christmas
sock bag together. It was a touching sight to see parents patiently guiding
their children on how to thread a needle and stitch the fabric, creating
not only a unique keepsake but also precious moments of bonding.</p>
<p>The event concluded successfully with overwhelmingly positive feedback
from both parents and children. This fantastic day would not have been
possible without the hard work and dedication of our PSG members, especially
Mdm Priscilla Quek for coordinating with Dart Zone to provide the Nerf
guns, and Mdm Coleen Yuan for leading the craft workshop.</p>
<p>A huge thank you to our PSG for making this event such a resounding success.
We look forward to more collaborative events in the future!</p>
