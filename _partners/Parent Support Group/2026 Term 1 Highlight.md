<h4>AIPS Parent Support Group Term 1 Highlights</h4>
<p>Term 1 was filled with energy, teamwork, and meaningful moments as our
PSG parents came together to support the school community in many wonderful
ways.</p>
<p>On 13 February 2026, we warmly welcomed a new group of parents into the
PSG family. Our school leaders hosted an engaging introduction session,
while our Chairman, Mr Sonny, shared a heartfelt welcome and briefed parents
on the school’s safety measures. The PSG Exco members also introduced their
roles and responsibilities, giving everyone a glimpse into how they can
make a difference. The session was not only informative but also a great
opportunity for new and senior PSG to mingle, connect, and form new friendships.</p>
<p>The festive spirit continued during the Chinese New Year celebrations.
Working closely with the Chinese Department, our PSG parents brought extra
cheer to the school through lively Recess Fringe cultural activities, giving
students hands-on exposure to Chinese traditions. They also supported the
performers with make-up and preparations before joining the school to enjoy
the concert together. It was truly heartwarming to celebrate the occasion
as one big family.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Behind the scenes, our dedicated Canteen Duty PSG members diligently
carried out their duties, ensuring students’ safety and well-being during
recess time.</p>
<p>We also kicked off our first PSG Storytelling Session of 2026, which was
a great success. Students listened with excitement and delight, making
the session both engaging and memorable.</p>
<p>From welcoming new parents to celebrating festivals and supporting daily
school life, Term 1 showcased the strong partnership and caring spirit
of our PSG. Together, we continue to build a vibrant and supportive community
for every child.</p>
