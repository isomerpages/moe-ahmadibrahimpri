<h4><strong>Term 2 Cyber Wellness Assembly: Empowering Students Through Awareness</strong></h4>
<p>On 28 April 2026, our students had the opportunity to participate in an
engaging and interactive live drama assembly as part of our school's Cyber
Wellness programme. At AIPS, we place great emphasis on Cyber Wellness
education, dedicating time each year to address topics that are most relevant
to our students' needs. This semester, our focus is on cybersecurity, equipping
students with the knowledge and skills to navigate the digital world safely
and responsibly. The performance, presented by an external vendor, followed
the adventures of a self-declared detective, a budding streamer named Kaijie,
and their friend El, as they navigated the challenges of trust, fame, and
digital safety. Students were actively involved in helping the characters
identify scams, deepfakes, and suspicious online activity, making the experience
both fun and meaningful. Through this lively blend of mystery, music, and
humour, students picked up practical tips to stay safe online.</p>
