<p>In celebration of Tamil Language Month, our Primary 5 and Primary 6 students
watched the theatre play Aduku Veetu Annasamy. They enjoyed the lively
performance and meaningful story of how to stay united in a HDB flat.Our
Primary 5 students also took part in a language camp, where they joined
various fun activities and learned to use AI to create proverbs. It was
a meaningful experience that helped our students appreciate the Tamil language
and culture while learning in exciting new ways!&nbsp;</p>
