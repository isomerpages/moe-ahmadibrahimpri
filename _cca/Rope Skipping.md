<p>Rope Skipping aims to help our pupils develop their acrobatic and creative
talents as they enhance their agility through coordination, strength and
endurance skills. This CCA also provides ample opportunities for pupils
to participate in National School Games competitions, as well as, school
programmes like National Day and Awards' Day celebrations.</p>
