<p>Our Indian Dance explores various dance forms such as classical, bollywood,
contemporary, bhangra, snake dance and many more. We provide various platforms
to perform which serves to promote confidence in self and team work.Pupils
never fail to display energy, expression, enthusiasm and commitment when
presenting the intricate footwork and dance vocabulary, and grow to be
resilient and expressive children.</p>
