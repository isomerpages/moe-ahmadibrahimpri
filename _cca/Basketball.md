<p>The Basketball CCA conducts its training once a week. Weekly training
not only focus on fundamental skills, fitness conditioning and game play,
it also emphasizes discipline and team building. In the pursuit of the
very best in each and every students, students will also have chances to
develop their leadership potential. The students strive to excel in learning
and refining their skills so as to represent Ahmad Ibrahim Primary valiantly.</p>
