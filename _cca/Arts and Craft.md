<p>Our club specially offers art making opportunity for students beyond the
art curriculum. Giving them the exposure to experience art in many other
ways. A great opportunity to groom pupils who are creative, passionate
and have a penchant for Art.</p>
<p>Our aim is to provide exposure and appreciation to various mediums, materials
and art forms. Also, to allow pupils to solve real life 21st century issues
using art as a platform.</p>
<p>Members are also given opportunities to participate in art exhibitions
such as the Singapore Youth Festival which will be held every 2 years and
as well as decorating school events like our AIPS Appreciation Day.</p>

<p>Our art club has introduced two groups: fundamental and advanced. Each
group receives tailored instruction to meet its specific needs. The fundamental
team focuses on building basic art skills, such as EOA and POD, and works
on projects that emphasize these basics. The advanced group tackles projects
that require a deeper understanding of art ideas and concepts.</p>
